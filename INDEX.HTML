IEEE

The IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers) is the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for humanity. It focuses on areas like electrical engineering, computer science, and information technology, promoting standards, publications, conferences, and educational activities. IEEE is renowned for its technical standards (e.g., Wi-Fi) and peer-reviewed journals.
